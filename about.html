About Us

Learnings from building distributed, eventually consistent systems that scale.

You can find us on Twitter or send us an email.

All Things Cloudy is a digital stage to share our perspectives, interesting learnings and challenges that we have overcome in building large scale, distributed software. This is an independent blog focussed purely on technology and there is no direct or implied association about the technical problems and perspectives of our current or past employers. We have been hacking and building distributed systems for years and want to capture our learnings, details of approaches we've taken to solve technical challenges we face. In some cases, the blog takes on fictional startup initiatives and attempts to build the infrastructure and software stack to support that initiative.

This blog is written under a pseudonym Mersenne Prime
